Services We Provide

We offer a wide range of services to improve different areas of your business.

  • Tax Consultancy

About Us

Historical Background

Our Firm, KOLA BAMIWUYE & CO (Chartered Accountants and Insolvency Practitioners) which was founded by Mr. KOLA BAMIWUYE, B.Sc (Econs), FCA, FCTI, CFAN, ACFE, ABR, ACIA, MBA) was registered by the Federal Government of Nigeria through our Certificate of Registration Number RV122881 of 9th November, 2010 We commenced business in December, 2010 at Port Harcourt as a sole practice under the able leadership of Mr. Kola Bamiwuye, our Senior Partner.

About Our Boss

Kola Bamiwuye is a graduate of Economics from University of Ibadan; he is a fellow, member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN). He is also a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria; He holds an MBA from Enugu State University of Technology (ESUT). He is also a certified Forensic Accountant with experience and other IFRS Conversion Certificates.
